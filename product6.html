Denon DJ Prime 2 Standalone 2-Channel DJ Controller

Denon DJ PRIME 2 is a 2-deck, standalone smart DJ controller with Wi-Fi connectivity. It’s designed and engineered for professional mobile, club and event DJs. Powered by Engine OS, this portable DJ controller doesn’t require a laptop. Its powerful processor can analyze music files internally, and on the fly. The PRIME 2 features a 7″ multi-touch, multi-gesture screen, extensive media source connectivity, 6″ jog wheels with color display, plus expressive EQ, FX and creative workflow controls.

PRIME 2’s 7″ touchscreen gives you a high-definition, multi-gesture experience, just like using a smartphone or tablet. Navigate your collection, finger-swipe to load tracks, instantly search through large music libraries and even rearrange your playlists and songs easily just on the screen itself. Change things up by flipping your waveform playback view from vertical to horizontal and also select and monitor a number of other essential DJ work ow features at the touch of a finger. Via the touchscreen you can also access PRIME 2’s Track Preview feature, for pre-load auditioning of a song and needle-drop searching through its timeline.

Access and play your digital music via the 3 x USB and 1 x SD inputs and even large terabyte music collections using PRIME 2’s built-in internal 2.5″ HDD bay. Want to record your DJ set for an important gig? No problem—PRIME 2 has you covered, able to record directly to any connected media source, even the one you’re playing from. Any changes to track hot-cues, loops and performance preferences are also saved to your source media drive for instant recall at a later gig.

Features

  • 2-channel mixer and 6″ capacitive jog wheels

  • 7″ multi-gesture touchscreen interface

  • Built-in Wi-Fi and wired internet connectivity
