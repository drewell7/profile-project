Pioneer DJ DDJ-FLX6 4-Channel DJ Controller for Serato DJ Pro and rekordbox dj Black

Updated with all-new features, the 4-channel DDJ-FLX6 controller from Pioneer DJ unlocks even more functions for your performance. With rekordbox and Serato DJ Pro integration, all you have to do is pick which software you want to run for free on your laptop, then plug in and start the show.

Keep the crowd on their feet with Merge FX, which lets you effortlessly switch between different musical genres. Whether you want to move from hip-hop to house, or rock to trap, the DDJ-FLX6 keeps every transition smooth. Full-size jog wheels with a track position display helps you perform intuitively and on the fly. Throw in other amazing features, like Jog Cutter and Sample Scratch, to take your sound in totally new directions. With added features plus four channels at your disposal, it's easy to play tracks on decks 1 and 2, then start scratching samples on 3 and 4.

Merge FX: Switch From Track to Track With a Single Action

Add drama to your sets and easily mix different styles of music with Merge FX. No matter what the BPM or key of the next track is, you can create an exciting transition whenever you want. Simply press the knob to engage the effect, then turn it as fast or as slowly as you like to build up the tension in the music. Depending on the effect pattern you’ve chosen, the pitch of the track might start to climb while a snare drum sample is triggered at shorter intervals until you’re ready to drop your next song. Press the knob again, and the effect is released to end the track. if you’ve chosen one in the settings, another sample will fire off. Or, you can hit play on the other deck to introduce the next song or genre of music.
